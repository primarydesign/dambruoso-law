    Overview

    Starting and/or running a business is challenging and demanding enough without having to address the innumerable legal issues that inevitably arise, many of which can appear daunting to a non-lawyer. At D’Ambruoso Law, we understand that through the eyes of a businessperson, there is no such thing as a “legal issue” – all the issues confronting a business are business issues.

    We are business counselors as much as we are lawyers, and are committed to minimizing legal jargon and taking a firm position, in order to give you the sound and useful advice you need as we guide you through the business and legal obstacles that you face.

    When you have a question, you will get a timely, direct answer, not a “black letter law” memo with a big price tag that will inevitably find its way to the back of your filing cabinet. As your partners in business, count on us for efficiently delivered, practical, and results-oriented advice to help you achieve your business goals.
